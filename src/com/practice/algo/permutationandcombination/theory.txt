- Permutation - arrangement in specific order
- n! / (n - r)!
- nPr
- eg. How many ways can 3 people (ABC) be arranged in a line - 6.
- ABC, ACB, BAC, BCA, CAB, CBA

- Combination - arrangement without any order.
- n! / r! * (n - r)!
- nCr
- How many ways can you choose 2 people from group of 3 people (ABC) - 3
- AB, AC, BC


***
- 0! and 1! is 1.
- if r > n , its 0, because we cannot arrange more members for limited ones.
